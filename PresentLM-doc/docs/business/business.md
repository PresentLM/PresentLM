## Pricing Model

Potential pricing strategies:

<div style={{ margin: '2em 0' }}>

| Model | Pros | Cons |
|-------|------|------|
| **Freemium** | Low barrier to entry; broad adoption | Limited advanced features |
| **Subscription** | Recurring revenue; continuous improvement | Requires retention |
| **Institutional Licensing** | Stable revenue; educational adoption | Longer sales cycles |

</div>

<div style={{ padding: '1.5em', borderRadius: '8px', backgroundColor: 'var(--ifm-color-primary-lightest)', margin: '2em 0' }}>
  <strong>Recommended:</strong> A <strong>freemium + subscription model</strong> is particularly suitable, allowing casual users to explore the tool while offering advanced narration control and analytics to educators and professionals.
</div>
