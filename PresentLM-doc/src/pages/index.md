## Elevator Pitch

<p style={{ fontSize: '1.1em', maxWidth: '900px', margin: '1em auto', lineHeight: '1.8' }}>
  Imagine a student revisiting a lecture deck the night before an exam, or a founder rehearsing an investor pitch alone. The slides are there, but the explanation is gone. The timing, emphasis, and reasoning live only in the presenter's head. <strong>Slides were meant to be spoken, but once shared, they fall silent.</strong>
</p>

<p style={{ fontSize: '1.1em', maxWidth: '900px', margin: '1em auto', lineHeight: '1.8' }}>
  <strong>PresentLM</strong> is an AI-driven presentation system that turns static slide decks into interactive, spoken experiences. Users upload their slides, optionally enriched with lecture notes or supporting documents, and an agent generates a natural, structured narration for each slide. Using text-to-speech, the system presents the content aloud while automatically advancing slides in sync, recreating the flow of a real presentation.
</p>

<p style={{ fontSize: '1.1em', maxWidth: '900px', margin: '1em auto', lineHeight: '1.8' }}>
  Unlike static slide viewers or text-based AI tools, PresentLM enables real-time interaction. Learners and presenters can ask questions during playback, by voice or button activation, and the system responds instantly, explaining a specific slide, elaborating on concepts, or adjusting depth on demand. Presentations become conversational artifacts rather than frozen files.
</p>

<p style={{ fontSize: '1.1em', maxWidth: '900px', margin: '1em auto', lineHeight: '1.8' }}>
  We combine generative agents, multimodal interaction, and temporal synchronization to support both presentation rehearsal and post-hoc learning.
</p>
